PTCR Overlap Calculator

Overlapwing

Predict the maximum number of charge reductions that guarantees no exact overlap among charge-reduced products from a precursor m/z isolation window and visualise the expected mass spectrum. Use PTCR spectra to inform inputs.

Inputs

Method

Overlap condition for a fixed precursor m/z window: r₁ / z₁ = r₂ / z₂

For a pair of charge states z₁ and z₂, the first exact overlap occurs at reductions r₁ = z₁ / gcd(z₁,z₂) and r₂ = z₂ / gcd(z₁,z₂). The window-wide safe limit is the smallest pair threshold minus 1.
